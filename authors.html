Marina von Stein (a.k.a Bt3 or B.)


I research Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and Security at Apple. Because science is fun, I am a machine learning collaborator at the Caltech HEP Lab.

Formerly, I was a software engineer at Yelp, I spent some softwaring retreat time at Recurse Center, and I was an Astrophysicist PhD Researcher at LANL, BNL, and NASA.

I am passionate about the applications of Artificial Intelligence in the perceptions of the human condition: being it in Arts, Music, Science, Photography, or Telling Stories. Back from my times as an Astrophysicist, I have been using pixels as a form of expression. I make films at the San Francisco School of Digital Filmmaking.

My family is originally from Italy and Germany but I grew up in a small town in Brazil. Because of my studies and my PhD, I have lived in several places in the world, including: Sao Paulo, Chile, Boston, Washington DC, New York City, and San Francisco. These experiences gave me the sense of how beautiful humankind is. We are all unique in our particular features, but very similar in our search to understand our presence in this reality.

Fond of shredding on a (surf/skate/snow)-board, skydiving, existentialist science fiction, playing the guitar, photography, figuring out how to make the future better.
