Hello, Human! Thank you for visiting my webpage!


Like several from my generation, computers are my thing since I started walking. I started coding back in middle school and during high school I even won first place in a technological fair. The project was named 'Medico do Lar' (Home's Doctor) and applied Artificial Intelligence to try to diagnose diseases.

I ended up pursuing a Ph.D. in Physics, interning in several National Laboratories, working in several projects, and understanding a little bit more about Life, the Universe and Everything.

And I am back to the dark side :). My career interests are now focused in several tracks of Information Security. Additionally, while in my PhD, I had some research experience on Machine Learning and Quantum Computing. Maybe I can use this for something useful in security? ;)

Some cool places I have worked/lived at:

  • Fellow at the Hacker School in Manhattan, NYC.
  • Ph.D. candidate at the University of New York at Stony Brook (Top 4 in Nuclear Physics!).
  • Graduate Researcher at the Los Alamos National Laboratory, in New Mexico.
  • Graduate Researcher at the Brookhaven National Laboratory, in New York.
  • Undergrad Student at the Large Hadron Collider - CERN School in Medellin, Colombia.
  • Intern at the NASA Goddard Space Center, in Washington, DC.
  • REU and undergrad at the University of Sao Paulo (the largest university in southern and central America; when I was at NASA I learned that USP is regarded as the Brazilian MIT!), in Sao Paulo, Brazil.
  • I lived on a backpack and a few bucks a few times as well: in Santiago, Chile and in New England. Freedom.
