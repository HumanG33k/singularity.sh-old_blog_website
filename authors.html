Hello, Visitor!

Like several from my generation, computers are my thing since I started walking. I ended up pursuing a Ph.D. in Physics (long story, it had to do with Quantum Mechanics and finding out how the Universe works) but then I came back to the dark side. :)

My career interests are focused in Information Security. I have some past work on Machine Learning and Quantum Computing (see bellow) and I have interest in applying these ideas to the field of security.

Here are some of my projects. Here are some of my snippets. Here is me at Twitter. Here is me at GitHub. OpenPGP: 145A 15D9 3393 4416 F7EA 0B9E E26F 1C8E 27D9 8506 (also at keybase.io).
