Some learnings I've got from living life:

  • It's going to end. My dad was murdered when I was in college. I never found out who or why. But I found how to understand the ephemerality of life and to appreciate every moment of it.
  • If you need a God, call it Time + Nature. All the answers are there. And everything can or will be explained with science and equations. Nature will recharge you whenever you feel lost.
  • Time passes much faster than you can understand. Time is a currency. Everything is already past. Our biggest mistake is wasting time on anything that does not belong to who we are.
  • Kindness is wisdom. And it is the most human response to our inability to control anything.
  • People will be rude. But they are just scared. Find yourself and let them find themselves.
  • Use money as your freedom pass. Too few money can make you stronger. Too much money is your prison. I had a poor childhood back in Brazil and this had a huge consequence on the way I see life and I fight for my dreams. I still try to not get too comfortable.
